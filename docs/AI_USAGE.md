# AI Usage Disclosure (Level 2)

## Course
Skills for Data Analysts (SKIB353) — Group Research Project  
MSc IT for Business Data Analytics — IBS Budapest

## Permitted level
AI Level 2: idea generation and structuring support only.

## How AI tools were used
AI tools were used only to:
- brainstorm themes and survey angles
- help outline a report/presentation structure

## How AI tools were not used
AI tools were not used to:
- generate final report wording
- perform statistical analysis
- produce results, charts, or interpretations

All analysis, visuals, and conclusions were produced and verified by the project team.
